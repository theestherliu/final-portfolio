Product Designer, Visual Designer

Girls Rising is a non-profit organization in San Diego that provides mentorship and career readiness support to underserved girls and young women. My team redesigned and rebranded their website to increase nonprofit awareness in San Diego and showcase the meaningful relationships that are forged within their inclusive network of female mentors.

UX Designer

Under Design for America’s (DFA) To Be Designed program, my team partnered with a San Diego-based non-profit organization, Second Chance, to redesign their website for their youth program, the Youth Garden, and consolidate a marketing plan.

Industrial Designer

One Village Philippines (OVP) is a project under UC San Diego’s program Global Teams in Engineering Service (Global TIES), which connects student teams with non-governmental organizations (NGOs) or non-profit organizations to co-create solutions to problems identified within communities around the world. My team and I developed a solar-powered, mobile-charging Cart on Wheels (COW) to combat unreliable charging and natural disasters in a Filipino village.
